Arsh Traders was born in 2006 as a new kind of surgical distributorship firm.

It was founded by Mr. Gunjan Chuttani who serves as the director of the firm, with the goal of creating a firm which becomes the centerpiece of the Indian medical and surgical field and terraform it into an unimaginable area.

We believe in quality products

We bring to you the best product lines from industrially pioneering countries like USA, Germany, UK and others to bring you the best product to facilitate all your high end medical needs.

We take a on-field approach

Our field personnel and employees are spread densely around Madhya Pradesh and regualrly reach out to you to give you the best possible prices in the best possible product line to fulfill your products needs which you certainly deserve.

Our approach

Underlying all our work, Arsh Traders work with three clear ideas and principles:

Independence
I

We do not follow the herd and nor are we deliberately contrarian distributors. We are fiercely independent and have a successful record of pursuing medical lines, ranges and initiatives we think make sense irrespective of whether or not others are doing the same.

Judgment
II

Succeeding in surgical and medical industry requires consistently good judgment – we achieve this by gathering the best available information and undertaking intensive, proprietary analysis that ensures our decisions are made with as much clarity and benefits for everyone as possible.

Conviction
III

Once we have determined a strategy or product theme, we pursue it vigorously, relentlessly and with real passion.
